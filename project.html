Inventory Management

Inventory management is the process of overseeing and controlling a company's inventory or stock of goods and materials. It involves the planning, acquisition, storage, tracking, and disposal of items that a business uses to produce goods or provide services. Effective inventory management is crucial for businesses of all sizes, as it directly impacts operational efficiency, customer satisfaction, and financial performance.

Warehouse Management

Warehouse management is the comprehensive process of planning, organizing, and controlling all activities within a warehouse or distribution center. Its primary goal is to efficiently and effectively manage the flow of goods, from the arrival of products at the warehouse to their storage, picking, packing, and eventual shipping to customers or other locations.
